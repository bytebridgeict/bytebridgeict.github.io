About Us

About ByteBridge

ByteBridge is a registered ICT company based in Lilongwe Area 18, Malawi. We bridge the gap between digital potential and real-world results — making technology accessible, reliable, and practical for businesses, institutions, and individuals.

Founded by Daniel Nkosi (Software Development & Systems) and Shalom Ghambi (Networking & Hardware), ByteBridge uniquely combines software engineering, network infrastructure, and ICT hardware supply under one roof.

We serve small businesses, schools, clinics, NGOs, and corporate clients throughout Malawi, committed to delivering innovative ICT solutions that drive efficiency, growth, and digital transformation.
